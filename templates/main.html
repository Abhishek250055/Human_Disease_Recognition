About The Project

Automatic disease detection using radiological data involves using artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to analyze medical images and identify patterns or abnormalities that may indicate the presence of a particular disease or condition. This approach can aid in the early detection and diagnosis of diseases, potentially improving patient outcomes and reducing healthcare costs.
