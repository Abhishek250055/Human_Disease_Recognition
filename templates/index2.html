About ML Model

A machine learning model can be trained to assist radiologists in the detection of COVID-19 from chest X-ray images. The model can be trained on a dataset of chest X-ray images with labeled ground truth, i.e., images that have been manually annotated by radiologists.

Remedies and Prescription

  1. Keep physical distance of at least 1 metre from others.
  2. Avoid crowds and close contact.
  3. Clean your hands frequently with alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.
  4. Cover your mouth and nose with a bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze.
  5. If you develop symptoms or test positive for COVID-19, self-isolate until you recover.
